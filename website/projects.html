Golden Orb (Senior High Project)

Fighting game project made in the M.U.G.E.N. game engine. I served as the group leader,
I drew the sprites and background, and binded the controls. The rest edited the sprites for usability and added music.
The mechanics of the game however is the default game mechanics of M.U.G.E.N.
we didn't change the physics or math in the game, we just coded in our own original characters.
